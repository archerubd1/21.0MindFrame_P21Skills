What is an Algorithm?
An algorithm is a set of rules used by computers to make decisions. Social media uses algorithms to decide what content you see and what gets recommended to you.
What you see
Content shown in your feed
Likes ❤️
Based on your engagement
Searches 🔍
Your search history
Watch time ⏱️
Time spent on content
How it works: Algorithms analyze your behavior continuously and personalize your feed based on your interactions.
Example: If you watch travel videos, you'll see more travel content
What gets recommended
Content suggestions based on your interests
