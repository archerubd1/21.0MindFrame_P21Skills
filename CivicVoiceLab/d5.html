Digital Activism Fundamentals
Step 1 – Identify Social Issues

Research and select local or global causes aligned with personal values and interests.

Step 2 – Platform Selection

Choose appropriate digital channels like social media, blogs, or petitions for target audience.

Step 3 – Campaign Design

Craft compelling messages, hashtags, and visuals to drive awareness and community mobilization.

Step 4 – Engagement Strategy

Foster community dialogue, amplify diverse voices, and measure overall campaign impact.

Step 5 – Iterate & Optimize

Analyze feedback, refine messaging, and scale successful initiatives for greater reach.
