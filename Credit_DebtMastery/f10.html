Loan Comparison Framework

Systematic evaluation of debt offers to minimize total cost and risk.

Evaluation Criteria

⚠️ The Common Trap

✅ The Smart Choice

True APR

(Rate + Fees)

Looking only at "Interest Rate" while ignoring high origination fees.

Compare APR only. It mathematically combines interest and upfront costs.

Total Life Cost

(Total Payback Amount)

Stretching the term (e.g., 72 mos) to lower monthly payments.

Minimize Total Interest Paid. Choose the shortest term you can afford.

EMI Affordability

(Monthly Cash Flow)

Borrowing up to the maximum limit the bank approves.

Cap EMI at 25% of Net Income. Stress-test against income loss.

Flexibility

(Exit Strategy)

Accepting "Prepayment Penalties" for a slightly lower rate.

Ensure 0% Prepayment Penalty to allow faster debt freedom.

Decision Methodology

1. Standardize Quotes to APR

2. Stress Test (+2% Rate)

3. Select Lowest Total Cost
