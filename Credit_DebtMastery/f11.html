Borrowing Decision Rule (EMI ≤ 25%)

The golden rule of affordability: ensure debt never compromises your living needs.

Max Total Monthly Payments = Net Monthly Income × 25%

The Safety Zone

Safe to Borrow 0% – 25%

Sustainable. Leaves room for savings.

Financial Stress > 25%

High risk of default if income drops.

Prerequisite: Keep ≥1 month of expenses in liquid cash before taking ANY loan.

If EMI > 25%...

1
Delay the Purchase

Wait 3-6 months to save more cash or improve credit score.

2
Increase Down Payment

Pay more upfront to reduce the principal and monthly EMI.

3
Shrink the Loan

Buy a used model or cheaper alternative.
