50-30-20 Budget Model

A proportional framework for balancing immediate needs, lifestyle wants, and future goals.

Allocation Breakdown

50%

Needs (Essentials)

Rent/mortgage, groceries, utilities, transport, and minimum debt payments.

30%

Wants (Lifestyle)

Dining out, entertainment, subscriptions, hobbies, and non-essential travel.

20%

Savings & Debt Repayment

Emergency fund, investing, and extra payments above minimums (avalanche method).

Tactics & Real Application

Automate Execution

Set up auto-transfers on payday. Separate accounts for Needs/Wants/Goals prevents "accidental" spending.

Variable Income Rule

Budget based on your lowest month or a 3-month average. Sweep any surplus directly to the 20% bucket.
