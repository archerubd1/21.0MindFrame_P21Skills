Debt Prevention Strategy

Proactive systems and rules to insulate your future self from financial shocks.

Financial Guardrails

  • Emergency Fund: Build a $500 starter buffer immediately, then grow to 1–3 months of expenses.
  • Credit Usage Rule: Only use credit cards if you can pay the full balance by the due date.
  • 24-Hour Rule: For non-essential wants >$50, wait 24 hours before buying to curb impulse.

High-Interest Control

Avalanche Method

Pay minimums on all; attack highest APR debt with extra cash.

Smart Refinance

Only refinance if total cost drops (fees included), not just lower payments.

Stop the Bleeding

Freeze new spending on cards while paying down balances.

Defense & Protection

  • Credit Freeze: Lock reports at Experian, TransUnion, & Equifax. Unfreeze only when applying.
  • Risk Transfer: Maintain adequate Renters and Auto insurance to prevent debt from accidents.
  • Digital Hygiene: Unsubscribe from retail newsletters to reduce spending triggers.

Freezing credit is the #1 way to stop identity theft.
