Importance of Financial Decisions

Your credit profile determines your access, cost of capital, and long-term wealth.

Strategic Impact

  • Determines the APR you pay on every dollar borrowed.
  • Critical for rental applications and mortgage approval.
  • Used in employment background checks for finance/sensitive roles.
  • Early mistakes compound negatively; early wins compound wealth.

High-Impact Levers

100% On-Time Payments

Non-negotiable baseline.

<30% Utilization

Per card AND overall total.

Credit Mix

1-2 cards + 1 installment loan.
