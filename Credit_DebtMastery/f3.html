Applied Competence vs Theory

Moving beyond definitions to execute tangible financial outputs.

Applied Outputs (To Build)

Score Tracking Sheet

Monthly monitor of FICO score and utilization %.

Avalanche Paydown Plan

Specific dates and amounts to eliminate high-APR debt.

Borrow / Not Borrow Checklist

Decision framework for evaluating ROI before applying.

Budget Automation Calendar

Autopay setup aligned with statement cut dates.

Theory Traps (To Avoid)

Passive Memorization

Knowing definitions (like APR) without calculating cost.

Ignoring Statement Dates

Missing the "Statement Cut" leads to high reported utilization.

"Unpriced" Credit

Borrowing without knowing the total interest cost + fees.

Value-Negative Fees

Paying annual fees for rewards you don't use or redeem.
