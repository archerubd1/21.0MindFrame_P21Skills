Meaning of Credit

Credit is a tool, not an extension of income. It is a product with a price (APR) and strict terms.

Definition & Types

The Core Concept

Borrow now, repay later. The price you pay is Interest (APR) + Fees based on your risk profile.

  • Revolving: Credit cards & lines of credit. Flexible payments, high flexibility, variable APR.
  • Installment: Student, auto, & personal loans. Fixed EMIs, fixed term, fixed APR.

Usage Strategy

Use Credit When...

ROI is positive (Education, Tools) or purchase is essential & affordable (EMI ≤ 25%).

Avoid Credit For...

Discretionary items (dining, toys, clothes) you cannot pay off by the due date.

Decision Check

Before swiping or signing, ask the 30-Day Rule question:

"Can I pay cash for this within 30 days?"

YES

Swipe for points & security.

NO

Do not borrow. Document ROI.
