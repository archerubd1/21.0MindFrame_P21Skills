Credit Utilization Rule

The second biggest factor (30%) of your credit score.

Utilization % = Total Statement Balances / Total Credit Limits

Utilization Targets

Optimal (High Growth) 1% – 9%
Acceptable (Maintain) < 30%
Risk Zone (Damage) > 30%

Execution Tactics

Pay Before Cut Date: Pay balance 2 days before statement closes to report $0 or low balance.

Limit Increases: Request every 6-12 mos. Increases denominator → lowers %.

Spread It Out: Don't max one card; spread spend across two.

The Math in Action

Scenario: Limit $3,000 | Spent $900

Current: 30% (Borderline)

STRATEGY: Pay $300 before statement

Reported Balance: $600

Result: 20% (Safe Zone)
