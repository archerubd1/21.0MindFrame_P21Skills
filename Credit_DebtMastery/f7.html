Smart Credit Strategy

Tactical actions to build your score versus traps that destroy it.

Smart Moves (Do)

No-Annual-Fee Starter Card

Use a secured card if needed to establish history.

Autopay Full Balance

Never miss a due date; setup for statement balance.

Small Recurring Bills

Put 1–5 small subscriptions on card to keep it active.

Strategic Limit Increases

Request every 6-12 months to lower utilization ratio.

Keep Oldest Cards Open

Maintain your credit age history anchor.

Credit Traps (Avoid)

Carrying High-Interest Balances

Paying 20%+ APR destroys wealth creation potential.

Store/Retail Cards

Often have low limits, high APRs, and deferred-interest traps.

Spacing Out Inquiries

Don't apply randomly; batch applications within 14-45 days.

Cosigning Loans

You are 100% liable. Avoid unless ready to pay fully.
