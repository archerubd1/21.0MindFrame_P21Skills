Spotting Risky Messages (Phishing)

Phishing is when bad guys try to trick you with fake messages. Don't bite the hook!

The "You Won!" Trick

CONGRATS! You won a free iPhone 15! Click here NOW to claim it before it's gone!

www.free-prize-winner-123.com/claim

Too good to be true

Creates false urgency ("NOW!")

Weird website link

STOP & IGNORE

The "Account Trouble" Trick

Security Alert: Your account will be DELETED in 24 hours. Verify your password immediately.

http://verify-account-security-help.net

Scary threats ("Deleted")

Asks for password

Unofficial link address

DON'T CLICK

The "Unknown Friend" Trick

Hey! I know you from school. Can you send me your home address? I have a gift for you.

Sent by: CoolGuy99 (Unknown)

You don't know them

Asking for private info

Trying to meet offline

BLOCK & TELL
