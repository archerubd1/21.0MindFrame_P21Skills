Ask for Help from Trusted Adults

Being brave means knowing when to ask for support!

Your Safety Squad

Parents & Guardians

They want to keep you safe and can fix tech settings.

Teachers

Great for help with school devices or cyberbullying.

Counselors

Someone safe to talk to if you feel scared or upset.

You Feel Unsure

If something online makes you feel weird or confused.
