Handling Bullying or Threats

If someone is mean online, follow the "Stop Light" rules.

1

Ignore

Don't reply! Bullies want to make you angry. If you don't answer, you take away their power.

Pro Tip: Take a screenshot!

2

Block

Use the Block button immediately. This stops them from seeing your profile or sending new messages.

Cut the connection

3

Report

Click Report on the app/game so the admins know. Then, show the screenshots to an adult.

Tell the admins

ALWAYS tell a parent, teacher, or guardian if you feel threatened!
