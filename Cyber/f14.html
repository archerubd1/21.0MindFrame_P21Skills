Cyber Safety Checklist

Follow these rules to become a true Guardian of the Grid!

Keep private info private (names, address, school)

Think before you post or share photos

Use strong, unique passphrases

Turn on 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Check links before clicking (Beware of phishing!)

Be kind and respectful to everyone online

Set social accounts to "Private"

Log out when using shared devices
