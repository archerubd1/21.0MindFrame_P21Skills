Think Before You Share

Ask yourself these 4 questions first!

Stop &

Think

Who will see this?

Is it for friends only, or can strangers see it too? Remember, nothing is truly temporary.

Is it Kind & True?

Will this hurt someone's feelings? Is it a fact or just a rumor? Be a kindness spreader!

Is it Private?

Does the photo show my school logo, house number, or personal info? Keep secrets secret!

The "Grandma" Test

Would I be okay if my teacher, grandma, or parents saw this? If no, don't post it!
