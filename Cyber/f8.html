Weak vs Strong Passwords

VS

Weak Password

Easy to Guess!

Short (under 8 chars)

Uses names or birthdays

Simple patterns (12345)

Don't Use These:
123456
password
princess

Strong Password

Hard to Crack!

Long (12+ characters)

Mix of letters & numbers

Includes symbols ($%#)

Try This Instead:
T!g3r-Jump-99
Blu3-Sky#2026
PizZa$Lover!
