3-5 Random Words

Pick words that don't normally go together. A "Blue Pizza" is weird, which makes it a great secret!

Mix It Up

Use UPPERCASE letters for emphasis and numbers (like a year or score) to make it tougher to crack.

No Personal Info

Avoid your name, birthday, or school name. Those are too easy for strangers to guess.
