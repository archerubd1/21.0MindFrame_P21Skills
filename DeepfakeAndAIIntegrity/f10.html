Deepfake Red Flags

Uncanny Artifacts: Identify irregular blink rates, unnatural teeth rendering, ear asymmetry, and blurred facial edges.

Temporal Glitches: Monitor for face warping during rapid motion and observe inconsistent environmental lighting shifts.

Audio Desync: Detect mismatched plosives, unnatural breathing rhythms, and inconsistent background room tone.

Metadata Gaps: Inspect for missing EXIF records, stripped provenance indicators, and inconsistent compression codecs.

Context Issues: Evaluate improbable messaging, uncharacteristic behaviors, and distribution through highly unusual channels.
