Verification Protocols

Intake: Flag, quarantine, and securely log suspected media assets.

Provenance: Verify source directly; utilize signed media and C2PA credentials when available.

Forensics: Execute multi-tool detection checks; record tool confidence scores and versions.

Human Review: Engage domain experts and enforce strict chain-of-custody controls.

Decision: Classify findings, escalate critical risks, and communicate utilizing approved templates.
