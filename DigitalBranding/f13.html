Activity

Applied Learning

1

Profile Audit

Open your primary professional profile (e.g., LinkedIn). Do a quick 5-minute scan looking at the visual elements and key text.

5 Minutes

2

Identify & Fix

Find 3 things that need improvement. Choose 1 specific item (like your bio or photo) and fix it right now.

Action Required

3

Content Planning

Draft 1 solid content idea to post this week. Write down the topic, format, and main takeaway value.

4

Peer Review

Swap profiles with a partner. Give honest, constructive feedback based on the checklist criteria.

Partner Task

Review Checklist

Is the profile photo clear and professional?

Is the bio simple, clear, and focused?

Are core skills and keywords visible?

Is the profile free of negative/careless content?

Is there at least 1 recent, high-quality post?
