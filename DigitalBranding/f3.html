What is Digital Branding?

Your Online Identity: It's the distinct reputation and personal brand you build across digital platforms.

The First Impression: It often speaks for you before you ever meet someone face-to-face.

How It's Shaped: Constructed by your carefully curated profiles, the content you share, and your consistent online behavior.
