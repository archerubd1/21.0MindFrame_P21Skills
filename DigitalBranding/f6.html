Positive vs Negative Footprint

Positive Footprint

Highlights professional skills and core expertise clearly

Showcases relevant projects, portfolios, and academic work

Documents achievements and verifiable career milestones

Demonstrates consistent learning and a growth mindset

Negative Footprint

Features careless, impulsive, or overly polarizing posts

Contains offensive language or inappropriate visual content

Shows a highly inconsistent professional image across platforms

Includes public complaining about peers, teachers, or employers

Are you a Professional—or a Digital Ghost?

Action: Audit your current presence and align it with your future goals.
