Profile Optimization (Basics)

01

Professional Photo

Use a clear, high-quality headshot with a neutral background. Dress appropriately for your target industry to build instant credibility.

02

Clear & Specific Bio

Write a concise summary stating your role, key interests, and the unique value you bring. Keep it focused and impactful.

03

Clean Username

Maintain a simple and professional handle across networks. Avoid random numbers, symbols, or unprofessional nicknames.

04

Real Identity

Use your actual name or a consistent professional alias across all platforms. This ensures you are easily searchable and recognizable.
