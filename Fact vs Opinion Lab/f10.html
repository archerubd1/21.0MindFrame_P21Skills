Pause Before Sharing

1

Read Fully

Don't just read the headline. Read the whole story.

2

Check Context

Is it old news? Is the date current?

3

Identify Source

Who wrote this? Is it a credible organization?

4

Look for Proof

Are there links to evidence or data?

5

Compare Sources

Do other trusted news sites say the same thing?

6

Check Feelings

Are you sharing because you are angry or excited?

7

If Unsure

Do not share it. It's better to wait.
