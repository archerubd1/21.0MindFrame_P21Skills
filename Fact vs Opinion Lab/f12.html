Truth Checklist

1

Proof

Is there measurable evidence or data to support it?

2

Source

Who created this? Are they credible and identified?

3

Claim Type

Is this a verifiable fact or a personal opinion?

4

Emotion

Is strong emotional language being used to trigger you?

5

Consistency

Do other trusted sources report the same information?

6

Accuracy

Are the specific numbers, names, and dates correct?
