Fact vs Opinion Exercise

Directions: Read each statement. Is it a Fact or an Opinion? Why?

"The school has exactly 32 classrooms."

Fact Can be counted and verified.

"The new video game is more fun than the old one."

Opinion Depends on personal preference.

"Earth orbits the Sun once every 365.25 days."

Fact Scientific evidence proves this.

"The sun is beautiful."

Tricky Opinion Many agree, but "beautiful" is subjective.

Critical Thinking: What evidence would you need to confirm each fact?
