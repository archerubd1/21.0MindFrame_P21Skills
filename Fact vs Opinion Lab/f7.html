Persuasive Language

Media messages often use "power words" designed to create urgency, excitement, or pressure, making you act without thinking.

"Best Ever!"
"Must Try"
"Everyone is doing it"
"Limited Time Only"
"Secret Method"
"Act Now!"
Check Yourself

When you see these words, stop and ask:

What is the claim?
Where is the evidence?
