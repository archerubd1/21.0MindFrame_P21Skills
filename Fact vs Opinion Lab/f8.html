Feelings vs Facts

Emotional Triggers

  • Fear or Anger: Messages that make you scared or mad spread the fastest.

  • Pride & Belonging: "Real fans share this" makes you want to join the group.

  • Excitement: "You won't believe this!" creates curiosity and impulse clicks.

  • Outrage: Content designed to make you say "That's unfair!" stops you from thinking.

Awareness Questions

  • What emotion am I feeling right now?

  • What are the verifiable facts in this message?

  • Who benefits if I share or believe this?

  • What evidence would make me change my mind?
