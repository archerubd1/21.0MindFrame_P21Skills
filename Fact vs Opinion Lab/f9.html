Why People Share Information

Excitement & Novelty

We often share things that are shocking, funny, or brand new because it feels exciting to be the first to know and tell others.

Connection & Validation

Sharing helps us fit in, get likes, or support a cause. It signals to others who we are and what we care about.

Fear & Protection

When we see a scary warning, our instinct is to help. We share it quickly to protect our friends and family from danger.

The Risk: Sharing based on emotion without checking is the #1 way misinformation spreads.
