Claim Verification

An open-source platform by Meedan designed for collaborative verification. It streamlines the workflow of fact-checking teams by aggregating requests and evidence.

TOOL_ID: GPH-02
Gephi
Network Visualization

A powerful visualization tool for exploring and manipulating networks. It reveals hidden structures and patterns within complex social data.

WHEN TO DEPLOY:
  • Mapping the spread of disinformation on Twitter/X.
  • Identifying bot networks and coordinated inauthentic behavior.
  • Visualizing echo chambers and polarized communities.
TOOL_ID: WBM-03
Wayback
Historical Tracking

A digital archive of the World Wide Web. It allows analysts to "go back in time" to see how websites looked in the past.

WHEN TO DEPLOY:
  • Retrieving deleted social media posts or articles.
  • Proving a website changed its headline or policy silently.
  • Verifying the original context of a now-removed image.
