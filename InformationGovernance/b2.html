Introduction to Information Governance
Information governance encompasses the policies, procedures, and controls that organizations implement to manage their data assets effectively. It ensures data quality, security, and compliance with regulatory requirements.
Importance of Managing Organizational Data
Effective data management is crucial for:
  • Ensuring data accuracy and reliability
  • Maintaining regulatory compliance
  • Supporting strategic decision-making
Ethical and Structured Practices
Organizations must adopt ethical data practices that protect privacy while enabling business value. This includes:
  • Data classification and categorization
  • Access control and permissions
  • Regular audits and monitoring
Scope of Implementation
The program covers four key areas: policies, people, processes, and technology to create a comprehensive governance framework.
