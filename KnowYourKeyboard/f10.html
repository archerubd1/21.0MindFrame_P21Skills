Peer Feedback That Helps

VS

Unhelpful

Vague & Discouraging

"This is bad."

"Boring."

"I don't like it."

Why it fails: It doesn't tell the person what to fix or how to improve.

Constructive

Specific & Kind

Use These Power Starters:

"I like your idea because..."

"Maybe you can improve by..."

"One thing that stood out was..."

"Have you thought about trying..."
