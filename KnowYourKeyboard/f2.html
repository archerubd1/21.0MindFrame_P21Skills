What Is Tone in Text?

Definition

Tone is the "vibe" or feeling behind your message. Is it friendly? Annoyed? Excited? Sarcastic?

Why It's Tricky Online

Without a face or voice, we lose 90% of communication cues. Emojis and punctuation become our new facial expressions.

Quick Comparison:

"K."

(Feels Cold / Angry)

"Okay, got it! 👍"

(Feels Friendly)
