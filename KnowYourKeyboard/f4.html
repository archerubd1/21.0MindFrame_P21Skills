Golden Rule 2.0

"Treat others online the way you want to be treated."

DO THIS

  • Be clear, patient, and polite.

  • Ask questions before assuming.

  • Use emojis to show kindness. 😊

NOT THIS

  • Spam or mock others.

  • Post when you are angry.

  • Type in ALL CAPS (it's shouting!).

Quick Swap:
"Calm down!"
"I'm here. Let's sort this out."
