What Is Online Conflict?

The Definition

A disagreement that escalates in digital spaces—chats, comments, or groups—often fueled by distance, speed, and anonymity.

Why It Happens & The Impact

Why: Misread tone, quick replies, public pressure.
Impact: Hurt feelings, rumors, and screenshots that last.
