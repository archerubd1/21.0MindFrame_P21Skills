Virtual Meeting Ethics

  • Preparation: Consistently join on time and ensure your profile displays a professional name.
  • Camera Etiquette: Keep your video on when appropriate, utilize a neutral background, and maintain an attentive posture.
  • Microphone Discipline: Stay muted when you are not speaking, proactively minimize background noise, and use a headset for clarity.
  • Active Participation: Use digital hand-raising or chat to contribute, avoid interrupting others, and consciously foster an inclusive environment.
