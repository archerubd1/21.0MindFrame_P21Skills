Practical Demonstration

Best Practices (Do)

  • Email Subject: "Action Required: Q3 Report by Fri"
  • Email Greeting: "Dear Dr. Singh,"; structure with a concise ask and clear bullet points.
  • Email Sign‑off: End with "Best regards," followed by your complete signature block.
  • Virtual Meetings: Test tech beforehand; keep camera on; stay muted when not speaking; utilize the hand‑raise feature.

Common Mistakes (Don't)

  • Email Subject: Using "Hey" or leaving the subject line completely blank.
  • Email Greeting: Relying on generic greetings when the recipient's name is known.
  • Email Format: Sending unreadable walls of text; using slang or excessive emojis; typing in ALL CAPS.
  • Virtual Meetings: Talking over others; visibly multitasking, eating, or showing obvious distraction.
