What it is

A curated directory to discover open-access journals and articles across subjects.

Usage (student workflow)

Search by subject or keywords; then filter by journal or article view.

Search: subject

Filter: journal/article

Refine: year/topic

Bibliography (capture essentials)

Record author(s), title, journal, year, and DOI (or stable URL).

DOI • URL

Final thought: plan, test, and refine. Strong research is a process, not a one-time search.
