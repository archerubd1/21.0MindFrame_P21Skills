Query building

Boolean Operators (AND, OR, NOT, “ ”)

AND = narrow

OR = broaden

NOT = exclude

“ ” = exact

Operator

What it does

AND

Narrows results (must include both terms).

climate AND policy

OR

Broadens results (either term; great for synonyms).

teen OR adolescent

NOT

Excludes unwanted meanings / topics.

jaguar NOT car

“quotes”

Finds the exact phrase in that order.

“digital literacy”

Tip: Pair OR with synonyms, then use AND to connect the big ideas.

Examples (copy + adapt)

Student-ready

Narrow the focus

Add AND to reduce “noise”.

policy AND education

Capture synonyms

Use OR to avoid missing key sources.

(teen OR adolescent)

Remove a meaning

Use NOT carefully (don’t exclude too much).

python NOT snake

Exact phrase

Use “ ” for names, concepts, or titles.

“public health”
