Boolean Practice Examples

OR = synonyms

AND = focus

“ ” = exact

NOT = remove

Energy + policy

Use OR for alternatives, then connect with AND.

("renewable energy" OR solar) AND policy

Health topic (exact phrase)

Combine multiple AND terms + one exact phrase.

(adolescent OR teen) AND sleep AND "screen time"

Exclude an off-topic meaning

Use NOT to filter irrelevant results.

biodiversity NOT "video game"

Technical + ethics angle

Group options with parentheses to keep logic clear.

"machine learning" AND (ethics OR bias)

Tip: If results are too broad, add one more AND term. If too narrow, add synonyms with OR.
