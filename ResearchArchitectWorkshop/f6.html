Credibility check

Evaluating Authority

  • Confirm the author and their credentials (education, expertise)

  • Check affiliation: university, lab, organization, or institution

  • Look for peer review or clear editorial oversight

  • Verify citations and references that you can trace

  • Evaluate the publication venue: journal, press, standards, reputation

Quick red flags

No named author, no credentials, or no organization listed.

Claims with no citations, broken links, or unclear sources.

Strong opinions with hidden sponsorship or unclear purpose.
