Criteria checklist

Ask + verify

Currency

Is it current?

Check publish date, updates, and if the topic needs the newest evidence.

Relevance

Fits your need?

Match the source to your research question, audience level, and assignment goals.

Authority

Who wrote it?

Verify credentials, affiliation, and whether the venue has editorial/peer review.

Accuracy

Evidence-based?

Look for data, methods, citations, and consistency across multiple trusted sources.

How to use CRAAP (in 30 seconds)

Answer each criterion with a short note (1–2 lines).

If Authority or Accuracy is weak, don’t cite it.

Compare at least 2 sources before deciding.

Quick scoring (optional)

2 = Strong

1 = Mixed

0 = Weak

Total: /10

Record the score in your bibliography notes for transparency.
