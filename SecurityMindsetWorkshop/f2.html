Threat Modeling — Purpose & Definitions

Purpose

Understand what could go wrong, how likely it is, and how bad it would be—then act deliberately.

Outcome

A prioritized list of risks and clear, actionable mitigation steps.

Key Terms

Assets: Data, devices, accounts, systems, reputation, finances.

Threats: Adversaries/events that can cause harm (e.g., phishing, malware, theft, insider misuse, outages).

Vulnerabilities: Weaknesses that can be exploited (e.g., unpatched software, weak passwords, misconfigurations).
