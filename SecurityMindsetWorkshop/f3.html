Steps in Threat Modeling

1

Identify

Assets, data flows, entry points, and potential threat actors.

2

Assess

Evaluate Likelihood × Impact; logically map specific threats to known vulnerabilities.

3

Prioritize

Rank identified risks. Pick out the quick wins and high-impact fixes first.

4

Mitigate

Apply controls (prevent/detect/respond). Accept, transfer, or avoid risk when appropriate.

Tip: Revisit your threat model after major system changes or following any security incidents.
