Practical Tips: Threat Modeling

MFA Everywhere

Enable app-based or hardware key Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on all email, identity providers, and admin tools to block unauthorized access.

Updates & Patch Hygiene

Turn on auto-updates for your OS, applications, browsers, and firmware. Proactively remove any unused software to reduce your attack surface.

Digital Footprint

Minimize exposed personal and company data. Restrict permissions, limit file sharing, and regularly review public profiles for sensitive information.

Extra Wins

Implement least privilege access. Separate administrator and daily-use accounts, network-segment critical assets, and conduct regular phishing drills.
