Types of Encryption & Tools

File & Device Encryption

  • Full Disk Encryption: Protects entire drives (e.g., BitLocker, FileVault, LUKS)
  • Encrypted containers: Secure specific folders or volumes (e.g., VeraCrypt)

Secrets & Credentials

  • Password managers: Generate and store complex passwords locally (e.g., KeePassXC)
  • Vaults: Protect MFA tokens and sensitive API keys securely

Messaging & Email

  • E2EE messaging: End-to-end encrypted real-time chat (e.g., Signal)
  • Encrypted Email: Using PGP for advanced email confidentiality and signing

Good Practices

  • Use strong, unique passphrases for master keys
  • Safely protect and store recovery keys offline
  • Consistently verify contact fingerprints/safety numbers
