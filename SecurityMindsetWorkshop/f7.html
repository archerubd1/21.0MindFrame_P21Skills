Practical Tips: Encryption

Unique Passwords

Use long, unique passphrases for all accounts. Store them securely in a password manager like KeePassXC, and enable data breach alerts.

Backups (3-2-1 Rule)

Maintain 3 copies of your data, across 2 different media types, with 1 stored offsite. Always encrypt your backups and regularly test restores.

Device Security

Enable Full Disk Encryption (FDE) on all laptops and smartphones. Securely generate and store your recovery keys completely offline.

Key & Operational Hygiene

Rotate encryption keys periodically and verify E2EE safety numbers. Document recovery steps, and never encrypt the only existing copy of crucial data.
