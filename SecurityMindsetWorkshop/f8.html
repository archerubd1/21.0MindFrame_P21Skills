Crisis Response — Definition & Plan

Definition

A coordinated reaction to a security incident designed to:

  • Limit the extent of damage and data loss
  • Restore critical operations swiftly
  • Analyze the root cause and learn
  • Prevent recurrence in the future

Plan Essentials

Roles & Responsibilities: Clear assignments using RACI.

Runbooks: Checklists for common incidents (phishing, malware, account breach, data loss).

Communications: Pre-approved internal/external templates, legal, and regulatory steps.

Evidence Handling: Preserving logs, tracking escalation paths, contacts, and decision criteria.

Drills & Tabletops: Practice scenarios to build muscle memory.
