The Security Mindset 

Objective

Build a proactive habit of identifying assets, assessing threats, and reducing risk using threat modeling, encryption, and crisis response.

You will learn to:

  • Frame security as continuous risk management, not one-time setup
  • Model threats against your real-world assets and workflows
  • Apply strong encryption and manage keys safely
  • Prepare and execute an effective crisis response plan
  • Choose practical tools that raise your security baseline
