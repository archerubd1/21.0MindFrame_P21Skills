CRAAP Framework

A standardized method for evaluating the reliability of evidence sources

C

Currency

The timeliness of the information.

Is the information up to date? When was it published or posted?

R

Relevance

The importance of the information for your needs.

Does it relate to your topic? Who is the intended audience?

A

Authority

The source of the information.

Who is the author/publisher? What are their credentials?

A

Accuracy

The reliability, truthfulness, and correctness.

Is it supported by evidence? Has it been reviewed?

P

Purpose

The reason the information exists.

Is the intent to inform, teach, sell, entertain, or persuade?
