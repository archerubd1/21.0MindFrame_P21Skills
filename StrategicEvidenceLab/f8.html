Bad Data Patterns

How data is commonly manipulated, distorted, or misused

Fake Statistics

Fabricated figures designed to look authoritative. Often "zombie statistics" that are cited repeatedly but trace back to no primary source. Always verify the origin.

Cherry Picking

The practice of selecting only the specific data points that confirm a position while suppressing contradictory evidence. Look for the full dataset context.

Outdated Information

Using stale data to describe current realities. In fast-moving industries like tech or finance, a report from 3 years ago may no longer be relevant.

Misleading Graphs

Visual manipulation such as truncated Y-axes (starting at non-zero), inconsistent scales, or 3D distortions that make small differences appear massive.
