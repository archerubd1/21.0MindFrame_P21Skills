Decision Framework

A systematic 5-step process for evidence-based strategy

1

Define Problem

Clearly articulate the core question. Avoid symptoms; identify the root cause you need to solve.

2

Gather Evidence

Collect data from internal metrics, competitor analysis, customer feedback, and industry reports.

3

Evaluate

Apply the CRAAP framework. Check for biases, sample size issues, and source credibility.

4

Synthesize

Combine disparate data points into coherent insights. Look for patterns, outliers, and trends.

5

Decide

Make the decision based on the synthesized evidence. Document the rationale for future review.
