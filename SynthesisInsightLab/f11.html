Applied Example

Demonstrating synthesis in action: Screen time vs. academic performance.

Research Question

Does screen time negatively impact undergraduate grades?

Source A (Correlational)

Survey of 500 students found a moderate negative association between total hours used and GPA.

Source B (Experimental)

Restriction group showed improved sleep quality, which mediated subsequent performance gains.

Synthesis & Insight

Key Finding

Causal Pathway: Screen time isn't directly toxic; it displaces sleep. Interventions should focus on sleep hygiene rather than just limiting hours.
