Misinformation Types
Understanding different forms of false information in digital media
Fabricated Content
Completely false information created to deceive, manipulate opinions, or spread confusion about real events or topics.
AI-Generated Media
Synthetic content created by artificial intelligence, including text, images, and videos that appear authentic.
Deepfakes
Manipulated videos using AI to replace faces or voices, creating realistic but false visual content.
Context Distortion
Real content presented out of context to mislead about meaning, intent, or significance.
