SIFT Method
A systematic approach to verifying information sources
S
Stop
Pause before sharing or reacting to content. Check your emotional response and initial assumptions.
I
Investigate
Research the source, author, and publisher. Look for credentials and expertise.
F
Find better sources
Locate multiple credible sources to verify the information and get different perspectives.
T
Trace origin
Follow the content back to its original source to understand context and accuracy.
