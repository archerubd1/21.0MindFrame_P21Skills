Deconstruction - Content Analysis
A systematic approach to analyzing influencer content
1
Identify technique — Recognize the specific persuasion method being used (emotional, logical, social proof, etc.)
2
Define intent — Determine what the content creator wants you to think, feel, or do
3
Evaluate credibility — Check sources, credentials, and evidence quality
4
Analyze emotion — Identify emotional triggers and manipulation tactics
