Outcomes

Upon completion of the Integrity Protocol, participants will demonstrate competence through the following verified deliverables.

Certification Ready

Meeting these criteria qualifies student work for the Digital Ethics Badge.

Plagiarism-Free Proposal

Submission of a research proposal verified by similarity detection tools with 0% unoriginal content.

Citation Proficiency

Demonstrated correct application of APA or MLA standards in in-text citations and reference lists.

Fair Use Application

Correct analysis of the "Four Factors" of fair use for any third-party media included in work.

Reflective Integrity Note

A personal reflection detailing ethical decision-making processes encountered during research.
