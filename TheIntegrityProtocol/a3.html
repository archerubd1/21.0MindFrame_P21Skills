Why
Integrity Matters

Academic integrity is not just a set of rules, but a fundamental commitment to honesty, trust, and fairness that defines our scholarly community.

Core Value

"Integrity is the essence of everything successful."
— R. Buckminster Fuller

The Impact of Integrity

Builds Trust and Credibility

Establishes a reputation for reliability and honesty, which is essential for collaborative learning and future professional relationships.

Prevents Plagiarism

Encourages original thought and critical analysis, ensuring that your work is authentically yours and properly attributed.

Foundation for Success

Cultivates the discipline and ethical decision-making skills required for long-term achievement in both academic and career paths.

Protecting Creators' Rights

Respects the intellectual property of others, fostering an environment where innovation and creativity can flourish securely.
