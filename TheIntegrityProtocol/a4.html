Program
Objectives

The Integrity Protocol aims to transform how students approach information, fostering a culture where ethical decision-making becomes second nature.

Primary Goal

"To empower students with the tools, knowledge, and mindset required for responsible scholarship and professional integrity."

Strategic Goals

Ethical Handling of IP

Instill a deep respect for intellectual property rights and the ethical usage of others' creative and scholarly work.

Promote Academic Honesty

Encourage unwavering honesty in research, writing, and collaboration to uphold the highest institutional standards.

Legal Awareness

Develop a solid understanding of legal guidelines, copyright laws, and fair use principles relevant to digital content.

Practical Skill Development

Equip students with practical citation and verification skills to ensure accuracy, credibility, and proper attribution.
