Citation Examples

Comparing APA and MLA formatting standards

1

APA Style

Social Sciences

Smith, J. A. (2023). Digital ethics in practice. Journal of Academic Writing, 12(3), 45–60. https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxxx

Structure Key:

Author (Last, F. M.) (Year) Journal Title Vol(Issue)
2

MLA Style

Humanities

Smith, John A. "Digital Ethics in Practice." Journal of Academic Writing, vol. 12, no. 3, 2023, pp. 45–60.

Structure Key:

Author (Full Name) "Article Title" Container Title Date (End)

When to Cite:

Direct Quotes

Paraphrased Ideas

Data & Figures
