Source Selection

Audit your information diet by categorizing sources into three tiers of value to ensure signal over noise.

Tier 01

Primary

Original research, raw data, white papers, and foundational books. High signal, direct access to facts.

Tier 02

Secondary

Expert analysis, thoughtful interpretation, and high-quality journalism. Contextualizes raw data.

Tier 03

Tertiary

Aggregation, social chatter, and mass opinion. Often high noise, low signal. Consume sparingly.
