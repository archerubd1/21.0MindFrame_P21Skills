The "Who Said It?" Rule

Always check the source before you believe.

Trusted Sources

  • Known experts or scientists
  • Official news organizations
  • Educational websites (.edu, .gov)

Untrusted Sources

  • Random comments on social media
  • Websites with lots of pop-up ads
  • Anonymous accounts with no history
