Source Activity

Compare the clues. Which source would you trust?

Unreliable

"THE SHOCKING TRUTH!!!"

  • All caps & emotional title
  • No author listed ("Admin")
  • No dates or old dates
  • Blurry, edited photos
Trustworthy

"Scientific Study on Climate"

  • Calm, factual headline
  • Author name & credentials
  • Recent publication date
  • Links to original data
