Tools & Skills

Essential equipment for every media detective.

Reverse Image Search

Don't trust the caption. Upload the image to Google or TinEye to see where it really came from and when it was first posted.

Observation

Look closely at the details. Check shadows, reflections, text on signs, and weather conditions. Do they match the story?

Fact Checking

Verify the claim. Use trusted fact-checking websites like Snopes or major news outlets to see if the story has been debunked.
