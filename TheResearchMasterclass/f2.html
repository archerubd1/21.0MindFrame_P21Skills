Workshop Roadmap

Program Objective

Understand academic research

Know what scholarly research is and how to use it to support strong, evidence-based ideas.

Learn information evaluation

Assess credibility, relevance, and quality so you can separate trustworthy sources from noise.

Develop ethical research habits

Practice citation ethics and originality to avoid plagiarism and build academic integrity.

What you’ll practice

Finding peer-reviewed sources using academic databases.

Citing ideas clearly using common academic styles.

Paraphrasing and synthesizing without copying.

Tip

Aim for clarity over complexity: strong research is organized, transparent, and verifiable.
