Citations

Definition

Giving credit to authors by clearly pointing to the source of an idea, quote, or data you used.

Importance

Avoid plagiarism and build trust by showing your evidence and respecting original authorship.

Research trail

Help readers follow your sources to check claims, learn more, and explore the scholarly conversation.

Where citations appear

In-text citations inside paragraphs (author/year or author/page).

Reference list / Works Cited / Bibliography at the end.

Captions for figures, tables, and borrowed visuals.

Tip

Cite as you write—don’t wait until the end. It prevents missing sources and saves time during editing.
