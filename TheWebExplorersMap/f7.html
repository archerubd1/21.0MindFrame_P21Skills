Smart Search Tips

4 quick tricks for searching

Use symbols and clear words to guide the search engine

Be specific

Use quotes

Use specific words

More exact

Add details like place, year, or topic. Example: volcano facts for kids.

Specific = fewer random pages.

Use quotes

Exact phrase

Put quotes around an exact phrase: "water cycle". This helps find the words in the same order.

Great for names, titles, and rules.

Use AND

Combine

Use AND to connect ideas. Example: planets AND moons. Results should include both.

Helps when your topic has two parts.

Use minus (-)

Exclude

Add a minus sign to remove a word you don’t want. Example: jaguar -car.

Minus = “not this word”.
