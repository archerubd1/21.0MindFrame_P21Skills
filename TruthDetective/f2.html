What is Truth Detectives?

Truth Detectives is an interactive learning program where students take on the role of digital investigators to uncover the truth behind online information.

  • Investigate Content: Learn to analyze posts, images, and headlines like a pro.

  • Real vs. Fake: Distinguish authentic news from fabricated stories.

  • Digital Habits: Build safer, smarter online routines for life.
