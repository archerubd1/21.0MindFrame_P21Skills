What is Fake News?

Fake News is false or misleading information presented as news. It is designed to look like legitimate journalism to deceive readers.

  • Deceptive Appearance: Looks legit, but facts, dates, or sources don't match reality.

  • Intentional Fabrication: Often created as disinformation to manipulate or profit.

  • Emotional Hooks: Uses shock, fear, or anger to bypass critical thinking.
