Photo vs Reality

Digital manipulation is sophisticated, but AI often leaves breadcrumbs. Train your eyes to spot the glitches in the matrix using these 4 checks.

LIGHTING & SHADOWS

Check for inconsistent light sources or missing shadows where objects meet the ground.

BLUR & EDGES

Look for unnatural smooth skin textures or warped edges around hair and clothing.

REFLECTIONS

Reflections in eyes, glasses, or windows often don't match the surrounding scene in fakes.

AI ARTIFACTS

Count fingers (often >5), check for melted text in backgrounds, or asymmetric jewelry.
