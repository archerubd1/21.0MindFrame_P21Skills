Investigation Protocol

The ASK WHY Method

Headlines are designed to hack your attention. Before you click or share, interrogate the content with four critical questions.

WHY IS THIS POSTED?

Is the goal to inform you, scare you, or sell you something? Check your emotions.

WHO POSTED IT?

Is this a verified expert, a reputable organization, or an anonymous bot?

WHAT IS THE PROOF?

Where is the evidence? Are there links to primary data or just empty claims?

WHERE DID IT COME FROM?

Is the source URL trustworthy (e.g., .edu, .gov) or a look-alike fake site?
