SOURCE VERIFICATION

MODULE 03

Source Protocol Delta

Source Checking

Don't be fooled by look-alike URLs. Verify the origin before you trust the content. A credible source is the foundation of truth.

DOMAIN INSPECTION

Check TLDs (.edu, .gov are safer). Watch for mimicry like ".co" instead of ".com".

AUTHOR AUDIT

Does the author exist? Look for a real bio, past articles, and professional history.

CROSS-REFERENCING

If only one obscure site reports it, be suspicious. Verify with 2-3 trusted outlets.

Imposter Site
abcnews.com.co/breaking-news

"Celebrity admits to being alien in exclusive interview..."

Verified Source
abcnews.go.com

"Official reports confirm successful launch today..."
